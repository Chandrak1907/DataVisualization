Dataset Summary

  • Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) is established by the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act of 2010 (Dodd-Frank Act) with a mission to make markets for consumer financial products and services work for Americans. Financial crisis on 2007 left many americans with loans. Therefore, this agency was created to heighten government accountability by consolidating in one place responsibilities that had been scattered across US government. Objevtive this agency is to protect american families from unfair, deceptive, and abusive financial practices. One of the functions of this agency is to take consumer complaints regarding various financial services or products. These complaints can be logged here. Consumer complaint database consists of complaints logged by Americans from December 2011 till April 2015. This dataset is regularly update by CFPB.
    • I wanted to identfiy patterns in number of complaints logged across different states on geo-plot.
    • I also wanted to check whether any correlation exists among different types of complaints e.g. credit card, debt collection and mortgage.
    • And, also I wanted to identify the service with more number of complaints.
  • I used R to pre-process dataset. I also used python to merge state codes for geo-plotting
